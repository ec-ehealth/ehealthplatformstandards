StructureDefinition: BeMyCareNetEAgreementMessageHeader

This is the generic MessageHeader profile for use in the different eAgreement flows from MyCareNet.

The official URL for this profile is: 

https://www.ehealth.fgov.be/standards/fhir/StructureDefinition/be-mycareneteagreementmessageheader
